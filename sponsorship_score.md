# Sponsorship Value Score (0–100)

The score is calculated using four components:

1. **Engagement Quality (40%)**  
   - Formula: (likes + comments) / followers  
   - Adjust for sentiment:  
     positive_weight = +10%  
     negative_weight = -15%

2. **Audience Credibility (30%)**  
   - Indicators:  
     - sudden follower spikes  
     - low comment-to-like ratio  
     - repeated bot patterns  
   - Score 0–1 manually or automatically.

3. **Content Consistency (20%)**  
   - Post frequency  
   - Trend of performance over last 10 posts

4. **Brand Fit Score (10%)**  
   - Keyword matching between creator niche and sponsor niche.

# Final Formula
Return score: 0–100.
